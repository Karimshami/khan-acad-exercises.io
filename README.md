    <ol><strong>Method:</strong>
        <li>Stir yeast in ¼ cup of warm water and leave it aside           for an     hour to become active.</li>
        <li>Add 1 tbsp of salt in flour, along with some olive oil         over     it. Add the remaining cups of water on it and mix         them together     into a soft dough.</li>
         <li>Roll out the dough into a medium sized thickness and          spread the fresh zaater oil all over the surface </li>
         <li>Place the dough on the baking sheet and top it up            with         1/3 cup of grated akawi cheese.</li>
         <li>Bake in your oven for about 6 min at 650 F.Serve              with fresh tomatoes and pickled cucumber.</li>
         
    </ol>
